Steven Paul Yuhl of Henderson, NV was Vice President of Triton Marine Construction in Bremerton, WA. Unfortunately, Steven unexpectedly passed away on Friday March 27th, 2026 of an unconfirmed heart attack while on the sand dunes just outside of Moab, UT. He was 58 years old.

Steven is survived by his loving wife Deneane, adult children Paul McLaughlin, Sarah Radke and Michael Yuhl, and his parents Michael & Collen Yuhl of Bellevue. He is also survived by siblings Brian Yuhl (IHS '81) of Snoqualmie, Doug Yuhl (IHS '82) of Mt. Vernon, and Pam Wells (SHS '90/91) of Kent. Steve was preceded in death by his oldest brother, [Scott Yuhl](https://ihsmemorial.org/class-of-1979/scott-michael-yuhl/) (IHS '79).

There is a void left in this world without Steve that can never be replaced. He is deeply missed everyday.
