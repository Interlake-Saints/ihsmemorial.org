Jose Miguel was an incredible student who graduated from Interlake in 2020 with excellent grades, and he worked for a year at Stevenson Elementary, where the students affectionately called him "Maestro José." Jose was a devoted son and brother to his three sisters, Janet, Corvus, and Jessica. He was a hard-working employee, respected as a manager at Safeway and a team lead at Pepsi, always committed to being an outstanding role model for his coworkers.

Jose loved playing video games, spending time with his dog Juuzou, and helping his sisters with their homework. He was a vital part of our family, contributing to our household and always sharing his love and support. His sudden passing has left a deep void in our lives, but his love for his family will continue to grow in our hearts.

Sadly, Jose Miguel passed away unexpectedly on June 5, 2026. He was only 24 years old.
