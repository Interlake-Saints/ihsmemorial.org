Scott, a superintendent at Skaar Construction, died suddenly on Sept. 25, 2010 of natural causes. He was 49 years old.

Unfortunately, Scott is not the only member of his family on this page. His younger brother [Steve](https://ihsmemorial.org/class-of-1985/steven-paul-yuhl/) passed away in 2026.

An article about Scott can be found [here](https://www.djc.com/news/co/12022199.html).
