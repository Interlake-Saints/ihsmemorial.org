Jackie Prentice taught Spanish at Queen Anne High School in Seattle and also English as a Second Language (ESL) at Interlake in the mid to late 90s, where she was cherished by her colleagues and respected by her students. She also served as a substitute in the Bellevue School District, where she left her mark on many young lives over the years.

She passed away on August 19th, 2025, at the age of 85.
